Highlights

Expert Talks

See and learn the details of what industry leaders and SMEs are building on the Grafana cloud and how they are leveraging Grafana services for the future!

Hands On

Instructor-led hands-on Grafana demo projects to learn how to build real-world applications using Grafana services and leading technologies.

Quiz On!

Participate in all-day-long interesting Grafana quizzes, that puts your cloud knowledge to the test. Top the leaderboard to win amazing prizes!

Networking

An excellent platform for professionals to connect and network with like-minded individuals, industry experts, Grafana and potential collaborators.

About Us

Amplifying Grafana Skills Through Communities

If you want to challenge the rules and redefine the possibilities of cloud technology, Grafana User Groups are the place to be. Join a vibrant community of cloud builders who are united by their desire to share technology experience and build skills in your city.

You can amplify your Grafana knowledge, collaborate with leaders and peers, and even make friends—all within an open, safe environment that encourages free thinking and diverse viewpoints. We are currently at 5,000+ members through all channels!
